The spaceship is in motion in the region bounded by the walls.
(You will need to press the "Start" button to start the rocket moving.)
    KICK: fires engine briefly kicking ship in direction it is heading.
    ROTATE: reaims ship, rotating it 45 degrees to left.
Rotate as often as you wish, but you have only one kick.
Objective: maneuver ship around corner to the finish line.
If you hit the walls, you crash.
The strength of the kick has been adjusted relative to the initial speed.
